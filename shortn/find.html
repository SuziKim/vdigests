Instructions

  1. Using your cursor, highlight an area that can be shortened without changing the meaning of the paragraph.
  2. Click Record Area to record the area of text you selected. This will highlight your text in yellow.
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until there are no more areas to shorten.
  4. When you are finished, click Submit. This will open the Output Textbox.
  5. Copy the text in Output Textbox and paste it into the Output Textbox area on the mechanical turk submission form.

NOTE: If you make a mistake, you may clear your work by pressing "Clear All" at any time. Ignore special character symbols such as {LG}.
