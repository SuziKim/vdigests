Instructions

You will select one or more phrases that best summarize the key ideas of the text that appears under "Your Text". Each phrase you select must be an entire sentence, or a shorter phrase contained within a sentence. Instructions and an example are below.

  1. Using your cursor, highlight one phrase that best summarizes the text.
  2. Click Record Phrase to record the phrase you selected. This will highlight the phrase in yellow.
  3. Optionally, repeat steps 1 and 2 to select more phrases that best summarize the text.
  4. Click Submit. This will open the Output Textbox.
  5. Copy the text in Output Textbox and paste it into the Output Textbox area on the mechanical turk submission form.

NOTE: If you make a mistake, you may clear your work by pressing "Clear All" at any time. Ignore special character symbols such as {LG}.
