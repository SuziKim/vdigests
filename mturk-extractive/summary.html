Instructions

You will select 2 sentences that best summarize the information in the paragraph.

  1. Using your cursor, highlight one sentence that best summarizes the paragraph.
  2. Click Record Sentence to record the sentence you selected. This will highlight the sentence in yellow.
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 to select a second sentence that best summarizes the paragraph.
  4. Click Submit. This will open the Output Textbox.
  5. Copy the text in Output Textbox and paste it into the Output Textbox area on the mechanical turk submission form.

NOTE: If you make a mistake, you may clear your work by pressing "Clear All" at any time. Ignore special character symbols such as {LG}.
